Our Ideology

Started recently, our shareyourFY website is made for multipurpose issues as well as taking many problems in consideraton that are faced by a students in their first year of engineering environment and also to help them in terms of providing necessary academic as well as non-academic skills. This site act as a mutual gathering and helping environment between the freshers and the seniors, where seniors not only provide necessary academic details or study materials but also share their experiences to make life more simple for the newbies.

What can you expect?

This project is divided in 4 sections, all of which users can access in service page. The link for the same can be found either in the navigation bar or the link that lies at the bottom of the page. There seniors can add materials available with them and FE students can refer to the materials that are made available for them by their caring seniors. Users can expect available various subjects' notes, instruments necessary for Engineering Drawing that can be borrowed, variety of C-Programming codes, flowcharts & algorithms as well as links or notes that are made available to refer to non-academic studies that might help students to cover those things that are not in respective syllabus.

What is the Future?

At this point of time, this site is not spread over large area. In coming time, we would like to collaborate with different colleges and will try to ask them to made this known to their newbies too. So that many in need-of-help students can take the most benefits out of this project. This project is made in such a manner or ideology, that with time, more content/materials will be available on the platform. Because,
"as demand increases, supply should also increase."

Who are we?

WE are an enthusiast Web Developer team currently studying Bachelor of Engineering from SIES Graduate School of Technology, Nerul in Computer Engineering stream. We would like to share how we got idea of this site. We were just wondering and asking ourselves that which problem we suffered yet, then we remembered of 2nd semester when we had Engineering Drawing subject in which its instruments were necessary and being not able to afford in very less time, we had to ask seniors for it that we can borrow. From this incident we thought of not letting any other student suffer the same and from there it all began!
